Pacific Radiology

In 2016 over 40 radiologist clinics, including 11 Modalities, 56 Procedures over 6 regions across New Zealand and Australia, united to become Pacific Radiology, a new benchmark in healthcare.

Our task was to create a brand under which they could all proudly stand ‘better together’ - combining six sites into one customer centric platform to create simplicity in servicing specialist appointments for patients, referrers and funders.

Combined with a soft, caring, highly emotive identity design the web-site sought to make what could be a stressful procedure easy, and put patients at ease.

At the heart of this experience is “Connected Excellence” - the single organising idea, captured beautifully through the human star. Symbolic of the interwoven, unbroken stories and expertise that connect the group to deliver leading healthcare.

'World-class radiology, delivered locally' was the branding initiative, working as a vehicle both to connect our six regions into a network of reassuring experts and to sculpt our face to patients, referrers, funders, and the public. "Designworks was on-board early creating some exciting brand concepts, but more importantly, showing us how they would integrate and strengthen our philosophy of giving patients better care, in the places they live" - Teresa Grace, Communications Manager

"The team at Designworks did a fantastic job, coupling creative imagination to attentive client service to achieve an elegant, on-spec result we expect will benefit us for quite some time yet" - Teresa Grace, Communications Manager
