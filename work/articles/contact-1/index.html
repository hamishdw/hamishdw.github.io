Contact Energy

In 2013 Contact embarked on a category changing step-change. To be more than just an energy company. To be a company that understood the logistics and comfort required for Kiwis to run happy homes - and to switch on a culture to deliver it.

Contact is a contemporary brand expression. A bright, bold colour system that reflects modern, happy living.

Switching on a code, an innate understanding of humans being - so New Zealanders can live the way they want to. A soft power, a quiet energy that doesn't shout about it, but works quietly in the background to keep customers comfy. Warm is cool.
