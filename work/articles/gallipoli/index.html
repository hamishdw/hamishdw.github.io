Gallipoli Exhibition The Scale of our War

What better way to extend the massive collaborative talents of Weta, Te Papa Museum curators and content creators than an on-line digital experience of comparable immersive gravitas for Gallipoli: the Scale of our War.

Re-interpreting Weta’s eight meticulously crafted 2.4x scale humans meant upsizing online content that acted as a highly accessible, virtual exhibition, enticing visitors to experience the significance of the battle first-hand.

Award winning digital design creates a groundbreaking and memorable learning experience, while paying homage to a monumental memory etched in New Zealand’s history - WW1’s catastrophic Gallipoli campaign.
